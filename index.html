About

Greenway Specialist Handling grew out of years spent moving premium furniture for brands that don't get a second chance to make a first impression — where a scuffed corner or a late arrival at a trade stand is not an option.

We run a small, dedicated fleet built for the job: ply-lined, blanket-wrapped, tail-lift equipped, and driven by the same people every time, not a rotating pool of subcontractors. We handle ATA Carnet paperwork, mixed-load customs declarations, and multi-country routing as a matter of course, not a special request.

How we work

The same route, every time

01

Collection & protection

Every piece is inspected, photographed, and wrapped to its own requirements before it leaves your premises — not a generic blanket-and-strap job.

02

Documentation & customs

Carnet paperwork, export declarations, and country-specific requirements are prepared in advance, so nothing gets held up at a border you didn't plan for.

03

Transit

Direct, tracked transport with a single dedicated driver per route — no handoffs between subcontractors, no loss of accountability along the way.

04

Delivery & installation
